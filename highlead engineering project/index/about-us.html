About Us

The company was registered with Corporate Affairs Commission on the 27th September, 2024, as Highlead Engineering and Project LTD and our primary focus is to provide professional services delivery support in the area of Engineering Services,Project Management, Consultancy and General Contractor for Private Individuals, Commercial and Government (Public Sectors). Our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering quality structures, outstanding customer service, increased flexibility and greater value. Our employees and associates are distinguished by their functional and technical expertise combined with their hands-on experience, thereby ensuring that our clients receive the most effective and professional service



We build our business on a strong platform of prompt delivery of high professional quality services at a very reasonable time frame, our sincere dedication and results achieved were the driving force that led to our recognition in short time. The company has developed processes and standards which help to ensure that all activities are sustainable and deliver good results



Engineering and Project LTD major strategic thrust is to become a major player in the Engineering Services, Project Management, Consultancy and General Contractor in Lagos State and ultimately Nigeria. As a player in this relatively untapped industry, we hope to create conducive homes for middle class families as well as the up-and-coming prospecting investors and first-time home owners. We then intend to support our clients with first class facility management Highlead
